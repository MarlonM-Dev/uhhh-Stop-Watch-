Stop-Watch

This is a little Stop-Watch app I put together with JavaScript. It’s pretty straightforward—just click the Start button to get the timer going, and when you’re ready to stop, well, you guessed it, click the Stop button. Honestly, I'm not sure why I’m even explaining this... It’s kind of obvious, right?

But, uh, here's the thing... What started as a simple project somehow spiraled into something a bit more... complicated. Now there's a whole bunch of buttons, and, honestly, I'm not entirely sure how it all happened. I guess I got a little carried...well at least I learnt a lot while making this.

* Just a heads-up: please don’t go hunting for bugs while using this quirky creation. If you find any, I might feel a bit, you know, insecure... *
